Otter Monk

Race: Otter (need more interesting name?)

> natural water mages, can use magic to breathe underwater (self only, natural instinct, based on magic competence)

> Island society / under-water cities, race personality depends highly on location, either self expression and being outgoing / playful or focused on tradition and restraint


Grew up in social environment that valued fun and self expression ("as otters do")

Rejected this notion of his personality, as far as to suppress it entirely - will not be told what he should be

Wanders the world as a monk, trained under a different race, value self restraint, planning, few emotions and being level headed (as to oppose his upbringing)


Natural with water magic, but does not use it extensively

Uses Force / Physical magic, both to enhance and better control his body, but also to create force barrier to deflect attacks and protect

> force magic requires high control and understanding of one's own body (if used like this)

> magic of this tradition is highly limited in range, best used only on the body of the user or at best to create short range weapons

> creating stationary barriers and maintaining them usually requires a second mage to cooperate

> force magic is night invisible even in the physical domain (normal sight) and can only be seen when the environment interacts with is (rain drops, mist, etc)
