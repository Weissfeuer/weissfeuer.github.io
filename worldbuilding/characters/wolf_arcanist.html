Wolf Arcanist

Race: Wolf (find more interesting name?)


Kind of a high society snob, not overly.

Considers himself better than "impure" arcane casters, as he can cast pure arcane magic.

Will still cooperate with others and his sense of superiority doesn't conflict with working with others.

Can be insufferable.

Highly competent with magic and has formal education.

> Has a lot of knowledge of lesser known topics and most meta-magic techniques (siphoning, crystal creation, focus crystal use etc).


As a pure arcane caster can:

Cast any "impure" arcane magic, using formal instruction and learning.

Cast immensely destructive and unstable pure arcane magic.

Create and maintain portals to other domains (same location) either with high focus or with the help of someone versed in the respective domain.

> Unstable portals can be created to lead to other location, potentially dangerous and demanding a lot of focus.
