World

It is no known, if the world people inhabit is a ((planet)), a flat surface, or if it even has an end.

People do not act enough on a global scale to exchange these kinds of information.

Regardless, no-one has found the worlds edge, or traveled from one side and arrived at the other.


Astronomy

Of the three celestial bodies in the sky, the first moon is probably the most impressive.

Hanging directly in the center of the sky, seemingly no matter your perspective, it will take a sizable chunk of your field of view, just because of it's size.

It does not move, it does not rotate and seems perfectly still.

Markings in the moon's surface can be used to tell north, east, west and south.


The first moon has a presence both in the spirit and soul plane as well.

It is both worshipped and feared by all necromancers for being the largest known source of crystalized soul.

Looking at it directly with a necromancer's soul sight is bound to blind them for life, so great is the sheer radiance of it.

In the spirit plane it is also the largest known source of spirit energy, the spirit sun.

It is considered controversial if it should be called an elemental or not.


Orbiting the first moon, is the much, much smaller second moon.

It is much less interesting from the perspective of the magical theory, not possessing any presence beside it's physical one.

The moon is however important for the daily lives of many people, one full orbit marking one month of time.


Lastly there is the sun, either much smaller than either of the moons, or that much farther away.

It's movement marks day and night, traveling in an "8" shape behind the first moon and back again.

While behind the moon, is it completely obscured, the only light available reflected from the second moon, depending on it's position.


The sun is also known to be the greatest source of arcane energy.

It is theorized that the mass of it inevitably affects the physical plane in it's most direct way, fire.
