Grand Designs

General

If by the design of an omnipotent god or mage, no-one knows exactly, how the grand designs came to be.

While many don't even know about their existence, almost everyone is affected by them.

Religious people will attribute them to their god, how they are as old as the world itself.

Others will remember stories from a time, where some of the designs didn't exist.

I can thereby be assumed, that they did not all come to be at the same time, maybe not even from the same source.

Academics and doomsayers will talk about the catastrophe that would happen if any of them would cease to be.

They say that the designs are all arcane in nature, grand as they are, but flawed all the same.

Some exceptionally powerful or crafty mages may even nullify them temporarily in an area of their choosing.


Academics are aware of a possible connection to divine magic, and the possibly disastrous implications that might have.

The common language

The grand design of the common language is both a blessing and a curse.

Ever since coming into effect, all creature capable of language could speak it.

From the highest aristocrat to the most secluded cave dweller, all could now communicate on common ground.


While this brought societies together and facilitated trade, it also brought a silent doom.

Everyone could now communicate with everyone, and even children knew the language from birth, limited only by the concepts they could put into thought.

But at the same time the older languages died.

Since there was no use for them for many anymore, they were no longer being thought.

If you did not keep them out of tradition, or as a manner to keep secrets, people forgot about them.

This left many a ruin with ancient scripts, stories written in old languages, knowledge no-one thought anymore, forgotten.


The old, the wise, the educated now fear a time where the grand design of the common language will cease to be.

A world with no language, a world with no communication.
