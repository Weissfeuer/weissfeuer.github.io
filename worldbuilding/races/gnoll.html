Gnoll

Appearance

Gnolls are hyena-like, biped creatures, usually up to 8ft tall.

They share many characteristics with ((hyenas)), such as their fur pattern, head shape and diet.


Four general fur patterns can be observed, depending on region and clan:

((Spotted hyena)), ((striped hyena))/((aardwolf)), ((brown hyena)).


Behavior

Gnolls are naturally aggressive and brutish in nature, this is only amplified by the stereotypes other races have of them.

Even the most meek of gnoll will grow to an impressive stature with vastly more muscle mass than your average species.


While gnolls tend to be more aggressive and confrontational than most species, they are by no means dumb or mindless.

Gnolls are cunning, specifically in battle, well coordinated and strategic in groups.

While they usually die young and in battle, those that chose to live a more introspected life as the shaman of their tribe usually become wise elders.


Society

Each tribe or clan acts as one familial unit, while smaller units of closer friends are formed on an individual basis (usually 3-5 people).

Tribes have strict hierarchy, separated by sex.


Females lead each tribe and tend to be the most aggressive gnolls.

Their word is law, those who do not follow it, with actions or words, are swiftly and violently disposed of.

This also results in a strict separation of social groups, male and female gnolls do not mingle, unless it is demanded.


The leading female gnolls will regularly challenge each other's position in the hierarchy, the highest being the matriarch.

These confrontations usually end in blood or death.

Young female gnolls will be trained with this in mind, and those that show weakness or unwillingness die quickly.

As such, while the birthrates of each sex is about equal, females usually only amounts to about 5% of the entire gnoll population.


Male gnolls thus take care of any other role than leading.

Guards, scouts, traders, farmers, shamans, any other role has to be filled by them.

While directions from the matriarch are law, they are also somewhat rare, and thus many of the "lesser issues" are decided by those most experienced.


Most gnoll tribes are nomadic in nature, traveling from place to place as local resources dry up and come back in time.

The most common kind of gnoll lives in desert steppes or generally dry and rocky environments.


Magic

All gnolls are adapt at earth magic, their particular form usually depending on their environment.

Desert or steppes gnolls will use sand-based magic, to soothe or start sandstorm, or to blind enemies.


Fitting to their more aggressive nature, many gnoll tribes have a tradition of creating their weapons of choice from stone.

These weapons are usually makeshift in quality and summoned from the surrounding earth on demand, and later discarded as sand.

Even an unarmed gnoll is therefore never unarmed, particularly strong gnoll mages will even create and wield weapons far larger than what others would assume reasonable.


Every gnoll usually has their own preferred type of weapon they like to create, usually whatever they had been trained in as a child.


Shamans or other calmer gnolls will use their magic to help cultivate crops or make the land more fertile.

Particularly skilled mages may find themselves as novice healers and herbalists.
