Crystals

Crystals or Magic Crystals are crystallizations of magical energy.

These can be consumed by any mage to gain the respective energy for their own use, whereby the crystal will be transformed back into energy and absorbed by the mage.

Crystals come in many shapes, usually a generally vaguely round shape with tiny spikes extruding from it.

Their shape does not change their function and can be changed by simple refinement processes (turning it into energy and re-crystallizing it).


Crystals will decay over time, based on the energies in their environment.

Low energy environments accelerate decay and high energy environment can even cause the crystal to grow (naturally occurring crystals).

To a small percentage, the crystal will always exchange energy with the environment and thereby become impure with the environment's energy. Specific storage procedures are recommended if this is to be avoided.


Crystals can contain any kind of magical energy as long as the energy can be crystallized (such as arcane, spirit, soul or even force).

Usually crystals are not pure, as in they contain energy of multiple different spheres, this is normal as hardly any environment has purely one type of energy.

Most people consider a crystal "pure" if it contains the same mixture of energies as their specific sub-domain (like fire magic being mostly arcane with some physical energy).

However, crystals can be refined to have only one type of energy if it is necessary.

Usually any mage will just not (be able to) absorb any unwanted kinds of energy the crystal might also contain.


Crystals can be created by any mage able to cast whatever energy they want to crystallize.

This process comes naturally to many races, mostly in inefficient forms and can be learned by everyone as long as they can cast magic.

Crystals can be created directly from the environment, but many find it easier to use energy contained in their own bodies to create the crystal.

Doing so will naturally drain the body of it's reserves, this is safe for everyone not suffering from void mutations.

Many mages make use of crystallizing their own reserves to save them for later and allow their body to regenerate the energies on it's own.

Correctly applied this can make a mage's energy output a lot more efficient.


Special care has to be taken when storing a lot of crystals in close proximity.

Depending on the baseline stability of the energy, crystals might naturally try to fuse with each other or react and free their stored energy into the environment.
